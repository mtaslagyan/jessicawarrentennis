About Jess

Ms. Jessica Warren is a professional tennis champion who has been playing competitive tennis since she was 8 yrs old. As a professional player and elite tennis coach, Jessica combines her extensive on court experience with her off court training in championship mindset. She helps build self confidence in children and young adults both on and off the court.

To really succeed on the court and in life, mental, emotional and physical strength is required. Jessica is an expert at bringing out the best in her student’s physical strength, emotional tenacity, and mental acuity.

Jessica has been a private coach for some of the finest students in Bel Air, Beverly Hills, Malibu and the Hamptons. She is delighted to be bringing her unique coaching strategies and techniques to Boca Raton and Palm Beach, Florida.
